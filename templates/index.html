Our Mission to Transform Surabaya

Indonesia's Plastic Crisis

Did you know Indonesia is one of the world's largest contributors to ocean plastic waste, generating over 3.2 million tons annually?

In Surabaya alone, plastic waste accounts for 15-20% of total waste, yet less than 10% gets recycled. Where does the rest go?

  • Clogging our rivers and beaches
  • Endangering marine life
  • Creating health hazards for communities

We believe this crisis is also an opportunity — to build a circular economy that benefits everyone.

Our Two-Step Process

Reduce

Educate communities on waste prevention and sustainable alternatives to single-use plastics.

Recycle

Create accessible collection points and partner with recycling facilities to process materials properly.
