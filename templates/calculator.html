🌍 CO2 Emissions

Recycling plastic prevents CO2 emissions by avoiding the need to create new plastic from virgin materials. Each kilogram of recycled plastic saves approximately 1.5 kg of CO2.

💧 Water Conservation

Manufacturing new plastic requires significant water. Recycling saves about 11 liters of water per kilogram of plastic, helping preserve this precious resource.

⚡ Energy Savings

Recycling plastic uses 88% less energy than producing new plastic. This translates to approximately 5.7 kWh saved per kilogram of plastic recycled.
